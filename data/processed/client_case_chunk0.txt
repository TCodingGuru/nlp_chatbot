telcox is a mid-sized telecommunications company operating across europe. recently, the company has seen a significant increase in customer churn, especially among monthly contract users. the goal of this project is to build a predictive model that can help identify customers who are likely to cancel their service, allowing telcox to proactively take action (e.g., special offers, customer service outreach). key client goals: - understand what features contribute most to churn - create a reliable, interpretable model - build a tool that can be used by business analysts to flag at-risk customers target audience: internal data science and customer service
